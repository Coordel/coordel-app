Intro Top 10

We see the great teams, we know there is exciting work to do, we know you have great ideas, people are inspiring. The potential is out there and is so exciting.

Ultimately, Coordel wants to make work fun and help you get more done.

Coordel is web-based software that redefines the way you innovate, execute and reuse work and how you work together with other people. Specifically, you can more easily control your work and improve the quality of your output. Coordination is especially important now that most of us are dealing with so many different ways of communicating with others.

Get Yourself Going

Not quite sure how to tackle a project? A talented team is all you need. Coordel allows you to plan and manage efforts that have not been predefined (yet).

Tame the Work Beast

How much effort does it take to coordinate all your e-mails, calendars, to do lists, phone calls,…? Coordel is your go-to place to manage all the work you could and should be doing. It’s complete control of your professional commitments in just one window.
