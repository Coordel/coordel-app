Opportunity

Coordel is designed to offer you choice. The opportunity to choose the people you work with and the kind of work you do. How? By facilitating teamwork based on expertise.

Publish & participate in Coordel Opportunities

Don’t have all the know-how to get something done, or even started, yourself? Turn to the Coordel community to find the people that can help you achieve your goal. Simply publish your request for a specific expertise in the Opportunities section. Of course, there is a supply side to this demand. Use Coordel Opportunities to offer your know-how to those looking for it. The upside: you get to do what you love and what you do best, and expand your network.

Market your expertise

As you master your skills by using Coordel, you can simply save and re-use successful processes and how-tos as Coords. You can even market your specific know-how by selling your Coords in the Coordel store.

How’s that for a world of opportunity?
