Opportunity

Coordel is designed to offer you choice. The opportunity to choose the people you work with and the kind of work you do. How? By facilitating teamwork based on expertise.

Publish & participate in Network Opportunities

Don’t have all the know-how to get something done, or even started, yourself? Turn to the Coordel Network to find the people that can help you achieve your goal. Simply publish your request for a specific expertise in the Opportunities list. Of course, there is a supply side to this demand. Follow Network or Coordel Opportunities to offer your know-how to those looking for it. The upside: you get to do what you love and what you do best, and expand your network.

Pursue Opportunities with Coordel

Coordel is built by a group of professionals excited about creating a new way of working together. And we’re really only at the very beginning of the Coordel journey. Want to become part of making it happen? Check out the opportunities provided by Coordel and also see how Coordel helps you build your own business.

Market your expertise

As you master your skills by using Coordel, you can simply save and re-use successful processes and how-tos as Blueprints. Share your know-how with the Coordel Network or with others.
