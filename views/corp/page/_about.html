Coordel is designed to enable you to be as productive as possible...and will continue to push the envelope to increase your productivity so that you can do more of what you love.

Opportunity is unlimited. Coordel is designed so everyone can help make it visible and accessible.

Coordel exists to maximize what people can do, together. Given the opportunity, each person is capable of doing amazing things. Coordinated, it's hard to imagine what people can't do.

Transparency is accountability. Coordel strives to maximize transparency and accountability in the work people do together.

Everyone should be able to choose the work they do and the people they choose to work with. In today's competitive global environment, you don't have time to waste doing things you don't like or are bad at, nor do you have time to struggle working with people who don't complement your skills and personality.
