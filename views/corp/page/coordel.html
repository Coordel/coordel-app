Coordel Opportunities

The Coordel journey is just beginning. At it's core, Coordel works when it's a network of people coordinating their efforts. There are three ways you can become an active participant of the the Coordel Network.

  • Become a Coordel Advocate
  • Contribute your expertise to specific Coordel Opportunities
  • Invest in specific Coordel Opportunities

Scroll over and click the binoculars to follow a Coordel Opportunity. Non-members will be invited to start a free 30 day trial membership. Opportunities you follow will appear in your Coord list in the application.
