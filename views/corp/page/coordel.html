Coordel works for you. By making you more productive and by offering you more opportunity. But that is not all.
Coordel Opportunities are Coords that have been published by Coordel. Anyone who is a Coordel member can participate in Coordel Opportunities.

Click the binoculars to follow an Opportunity. Non-members will be invited to start a free 30 trial membership. Opportunities will appear in the Coord list in the application.
