Experts: Employees, Contractors, Consultants

For every Coordel Opportunity, Coordel allocates a number of licenses and agrees to pay 50% of the revenue generated by the licenses to the participants who do the work. That payment continues for the life of the licenses allocated. Here is an example:

  • Licenses allocated: 5
  • Monthly license fee: $10
  • Monthly revenue: $25 = 5 X $10 X 50%
  • Annual revenue: $300

Any Coordel member can participate in Coordel Opportunities. Coordel will allocate the license rights when the opportunity is completed based on feedback provided by other People in the Coord and the quality of successfully completed Deliverables. Allocations happen in the order the work is completed and as new licenses become available.

Investors

Looking to invest? Coordel allows you to invest in Coordel Opportunities. The amount you invest will be used to contract unassigned work in the Opportunities in which you are investing. Your potential return is the same as a time investment. You can invest in as little as a single license or invest across multiple opportunities. One license costs $60 and calculated by multiplying the monthly return by 12 months. Any membership that lasts more than a year will result a return on your investment.
