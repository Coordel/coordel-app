Coordel Residual Commissions

When you sell a Coordel license to a business contact, customer or just someone you know, you will earn 25% of that license. There are no forms to fill out or hoops to jump through. When you invite people to become a Coordel member and they convert to a paying membership, you automatically receive 25% of their license fee for as long as the license is active.
