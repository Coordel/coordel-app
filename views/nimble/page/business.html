Advocates

Are you passionate about people and their talents? Do you want to help people become more productive and seize more opportunity? Become Coordel-certified today. Play a part in growing the Coordel community and generate increasing Coordel returns for yourself, automatically and immediately.

Experts: Employees, Contractors, Consultants

Based on the amount of work required to complete an Opportunity, Coordel pledges an appropriate number of licenses. Coordel agrees to pay participants who do the work 50% of the revenue generated by those licenses. That payment continues for as long as the member pays the monthly fee on the license. Here is an example:

  • Licenses pledged: 5
  • Monthly license fee paid by member: $10
  • Monthly revenue: $25 = 5 licenses x $10 per month x 50% commission
  • Annual revenue: $300

Any Coordel Registered Member can participate in Coordel Opportunities. Coordel will allocate the license rights when the Opportunity is completed based on two variables - feedback provided by other People in the Coord, and the quality of successfully completed Deliverables. License allocations are distributed in the order the work is completed and as new licenses are purchased by new members.

Investors

Looking to invest? Coordel allows you to invest in Coordel Opportunities. The amount you invest will be used to contract unassigned work in the Opportunities in which you choose to invest. You can invest in as little as a single license or across multiple opportunities. One license costs $60. After one year, license allocations are distributed to investors in the order in which investments were made and as new licenses are purchased by new members.
