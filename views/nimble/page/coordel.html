Coordel Opportunities

The Coordel journey is just beginning. At its core, Coordel works when a network of people are coordinating their efforts. There are three ways you can participate in the Coordel Network.

  • Become a Coordel Advocate.
  • Contribute your expertise to specific Coordel Opportunities.
  • Become an Investor in specific Coordel Opportunities.

Scroll over and click the binoculars icon to follow a Coordel Opportunity. Non-members will be invited to start a free 30-day trial membership. Opportunities you follow will appear in your Coord list within the application.
