Forget formal evaluations. We’re talking about improving as a team, one job at a time. Coordel gives you the opportunity to share lessons learned when you’re done with the work, before moving on to the next one.

When a Coord with more than two members is marked done, the Coord is moved into a Done list for each person. It stays in their Done list until they provide feedback.

To provide feedback, each person is asked to “Indicate how much you would like to work with each person again,” a question that is designed to qualify how well individuals work with others and how straightforward and efficient they work.
