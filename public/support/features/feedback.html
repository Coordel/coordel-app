Forget formal evaluations. We’re talking about improving as a team, one job at a time. Coordel allows you to give immediate feedback to the team when a Coord is finished.

When a Coord with more than two members is marked "Done", the Coord is moved into a Done list for each person. It stays in their Done list until they provide feedback.

To provide feedback, everyone is asked to “Indicate how much you would like to work with each person again,” a question that is designed to qualify how well individuals work with others and how straightforward and efficiently they work.
