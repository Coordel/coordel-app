How much time do you spend looking for that e-mail or file? Coordel gives you one Workspace where you plan, execute and deliver your work. From the briefing to the deliverables, it’s all there, neatly organized in one overview.

Coordel’s Task Workspace is designed to help you manage your work. A simple click on a task title will bring you to its Workspace.

The top of the Task Workspace features the information about the task, including its purpose and the Coord it is part of. Briefing documents can also be attached to the task Workspace so they are always in the same place.

If a task has blockers, they will be listed below the task info. If a blocker has been completed, you will see the final version of its deliverables for you to work with.

Below the blockers is where you work on and with your deliverables for this task. When you get assigned a task, the first step is to clarify the deliverables. Click the edit button to add, update or remove deliverables.

The Task Workspace also allows you to define a required order in which a task will be executed. Simply indicate which steps within the task are blocking others. As you complete a step, the one it was blocking will become available for you to work on in the deliverables area of the Workspace.

Under the deliverables area, you will see the tasks that are blocked by this one. That way you will know which others you are blocking.

Finally, you can plan what needs to happen to complete this task’s deliverables. The right column of the Task Workspace has three supporting tabs.

  1. Task Checklist – where you can make a list of things you need to do to finish the task. There is a mini version of the Turbo Wizard available for you to run on Checklists. When you have created a Checklist, you can use the ‘show checklist’ option in your Current list to see your tasks with their Checklists.
  2. Notes – where you keep your notes about a task.
  3. Task Stream – where you can enter messages for the other Coord members.
