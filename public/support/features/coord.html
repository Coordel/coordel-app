Avoid confusion about when you will start your work. Set the defer date of a task to when you intend to start working on it. Your team members will be notified automatically.

When your Current list is getting too long, you can apply the “Getting Things Done” methodology by using the Turbo Wizard to trim it down to what is manageable today. If you are not going to start working on a task today, you can defer it. All tasks you have deferred to a later date can be viewed and accessed in the Deferred list. When the defer date arrives, the task will automatically move back to your Current list

Unsurprisingly, Coordel is built on Coords. Coords are how you go from setting to achieving a goal through a series of tasks executed by team of experts. Coords bring communication, agreements, productivity, transparency and accountability to the process.

Coords enable you to get work started, and completed, with one or more other people in just a few clicks.

When you start a Coord, you will have the following options to complete. No fields are mandatory but some default to values when not provided:

  1. Name - enter the name of this Coord (defaults to 'No Name')
  2. Purpose - enter what you want to accomplish
  3. Deadline - enter the deadline of the Coord. All tasks in the Coord will default to this deadline (defaults to seven days from now)
  4. Responsible - enter the person responsible for the successful completion of the tasks in the Coord (defaults to you)
  5. People - enter any people who will be invited to follow the Coord
  6. Roles - enter any people who will be invited to participate in the Coord
  • Role Name - enter the name of the Role this participant will have
  • Person - enter the name of a Person entered in People
  1. Defer - enter the date when the Coord will start
  2. Attachments - upload any files that everyone in the Coord will need like templates, samples, etc

When you need to accomplish something, but aren’t exactly sure how, just start a Coord. Add team members with the skills you need and use the Stream to discuss how to meet your objective. The task or series of tasks that are needed to reach the goal will emerge from your back-and-forth with these experts.

Coords are designed to coordinate people, to distribute the authority and responsibility needed for everyone to be highly productive and to automatically make progress transparent.
