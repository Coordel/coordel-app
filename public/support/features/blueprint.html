Coordel helps you get the most out of your expertise. When you have captured a job well done in specific tasks or Coords, Coordel lets you blueprint and re-use them with just one click.

Whereas Coords are designed to help you get started and manage the work required to reach your goals, Blueprints are there to make sure you can repeat successful Coords. You can also blueprint tasks that have worked well for you.

Just click on the "Blueprint" icon and you are ready to re-use the task or Coord. Coordel automatically calculates a new deadline based on the time it took to complete it before.
