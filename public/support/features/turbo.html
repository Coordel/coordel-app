Don't get overwhelmed by the work on your plate. Turbo your way through your to-do list by making a simple choice for each task: do it, delegate it or defer it…. You are ready to start your day in 1-2-3 easy steps.

Your Current list contains all the tasks you have agreed to and that aren’t blocked, delegated or deferred. That’s a good first step in deciding what to do next. However, sometimes even your Current list can be overwhelming. That is where the Turbo Wizard comes in.

The Turbo Wizard moves you from the top to the bottom of your Current list, giving you a view of each task’s details. To filter your Current list down to what you can accomplish today, the Turbo Wizard provides you the following options for each task.

  1. Do it – as in “right now.” If you can do the task in two minutes or less, this option takes you to the Task Workspace to complete the task. It also shows a two-minute timer to help you stay focused.
  2. Leave it – choose this option if you will need more than two minutes, but you will get to it today.
  3. Defer it – use this button if you know you won’t get to it today. When you choose to defer, you will see the following options:
  • One day, one week, two weeks – moves the task to your Deferred list. After the selected time, the task will automatically move back into your Current list.
  • Other – brings up the Task Calendar, where you can choose a defer date.
  • Delegate it – allows you to delegate the task to another person and/or another Coord.

… And if the task is Private

  1. Someday – moves the task to your Someday list.
  2. Archive It – marks the task Done and moves it to your Archive list.
  3. Delete It – deletes the task.
